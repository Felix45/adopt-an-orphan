Zawadi Foundation
Adopt an Orphan Campaign

The Zawadi Foundation is a non-governmental organisation that provides a temporary home for children who have lost their parents. Our founders believe that every child has the right to be cared for and loved, therefore we not only offer adoption services but we also vet and interview prospective parents to see if they are ready to adopt.
